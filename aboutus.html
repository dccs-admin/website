About DCCS 学校介绍

Our Founding Story

Delaware Chester Community School (DCCS) is a non-profit school, located in Garnet Valley school district in the south-western suburb of Philadelphia. It was founded in Summer 2020. The purpose of the School is to teach Chinese language, STEM arts and sports classes, to K-12 students in local communities to promote the awareness of the Chinese culture and heritage, as well as to facilitate the mutual understanding between Chinese and other ethnic groups in the local community through educational and cultural activities and events.
