Employment

The Delaware Chester Community School (DCCS) leadership board is inviting candidates interested in teaching at DCCS to join its dedicated group of teachers.

DCCS offers training and development opportunities to all of its instructors. The following is a list of general area topics in need of instructors:

  • Mandarin as a heritage language
  • Mandarin as a second language
  • Math (Pre-MathCounts, MathCounts, AMC8, AMC10)
  • English Reading & Writing
  • Public Speaking
  • Debating
  • Robotics
  • Coding
  • Chess
  • Music Instruments
  • Singing
  • Dancing
  • Drawing
  • Arts & Crafts
